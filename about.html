Our Story

Founded in 2022 by college friends Aarav Mehta and Priya Kapoor, Matcha Mafia was born out of a shared love for matcha and creativity. Our goal was simple: build a space that feels like home—with the aroma of fresh brews, cozy couches, and great conversations.

We source premium, ethically grown matcha and blend them with care. Our in-house bakery serves fresh pastries and the ambiance is curated for creators, readers, and dreamers alike.

Whether you’re here for a quick matcha latte or hours of inspiration, Matcha Mafia is your third place—beyond home and work.
